About Morfeo

Morfeo is an investment analysis platform powered by Yahoo Finance. Data is fetched via an Express backend proxy. User accounts and persistent data are managed through Firebase.

Portfolio and watchlist data is synced to Firestore and also cached in localStorage for fast offline access.

Indicators Used
RSI MACD Bollinger EMA 200/100/50/25 Ichimoku ATR Ann. Volatility
Data Management
Removes portfolio, watchlist and settings from localStorage.
